# Privacy Policy for Prism Percent

**Effective Date:** April 2, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

NEXRAVEN INNOVATIONS ("we", "us", or "our") operates Prism Percent and takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information may be collected when you use the app, how it is used, and what choices you have.

This policy applies to all users of Prism Percent and follows Google Play's data safety requirements.

---

## 2. What Information We Collect

Prism Percent is built with privacy first. Core features work completely offline and require no data collection. The table below describes the limited information that **may** be collected — only if you give permission and have an internet connection.

| Category             | What Is Collected                                                | When It Happens                                                         | Is It Required?               |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| **App usage**        | Anonymous information about which features you use and when      | Only when online and you have given analytics permission                | Optional — your choice        |
| **Crash reports**    | Anonymous error logs with no personal details                    | Only when an error occurs and you have given error reporting permission | Optional — your choice        |
| **Advertising data** | Anonymous identifiers used by ad systems to show and measure ads | Only when online and you have given ad permission                       | Optional — your choice        |
| **Feedback**         | Any comments you choose to send through the in-app feedback form | Only when you decide to submit feedback                                 | Optional — entirely up to you |

**What we never collect:**

- Your name, email address, or any personal identifier.
- Your device location.
- Your calculation inputs, results, history, presets, or any content you enter.
- Financial, health, or other sensitive information.
- Your contacts, files, photos, or messages.

Everything you calculate, save, or set up in Prism Percent stays on your device and is never sent anywhere else.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

Any data collected — always with your permission — is used only for:

- **App analytics:** To understand which features people use, so we can improve the app.
- **Crash reporting:** To find and fix bugs, keeping the app stable.
- **Advertising:** To show ads and measure how they perform, which keeps the app free.
- **Feedback review:** To read and act on comments you send us.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

---

## 4. Your Choices and Consent

When you first open Prism Percent, you will see a privacy screen before you can use the app. You will be asked to make separate choices for:

1. **App Usage Analytics** — anonymous feature usage data.
2. **Ad System Data** — anonymous interaction data shared with our ad partner.
3. **Share Data for Advertising** — data used to improve ad relevance.
4. **Tailored Advertising** — personalised ads based on your usage.

**All four options start as "no".** Nothing is collected or shared until you say yes.

You can change your mind at any time from the app's privacy settings. Changes take effect immediately — no restart needed.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

Prism Percent uses Google services for analytics, crash reporting, advertising, and Play Store features. When you give permission, data may be processed by Google according to their own policies.

No calculation data you enter is ever shared with any third party.

For more on how Google handles data, visit: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

---

## 6. Data Storage and Security

Your calculations, history, presets, preferences, and consent choices are stored locally on your device using standard storage methods. We do not run any backend servers for personal user data.

When you have given permission, anonymous analytics and crash reports are sent securely over encrypted connections. These records contain no personal information.

Feedback you submit is stored securely on our systems and also contains no personal information.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Prism Percent is a general-purpose calculator available to users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your region). The app does not have features aimed specifically at children beyond its basic calculation functions.

If you believe a child has submitted personal information through the app, please contact us at **contact@nexraven.net** and we will take appropriate action.

---

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

- **Access** — ask what data may be held related to your use of the app.
- **Correct** — ask for inaccurate data to be fixed.
- **Delete** — ask for data to be removed.
- **Object** — object to analytics or advertising processing.
- **Withdraw consent** — change your mind at any time from the app's privacy settings.

Because we only collect anonymous, non-identifiable data, we generally cannot link records back to a specific person. For any privacy request, please contact us at **contact@nexraven.net**.

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will let you know through the app. We encourage you to check back occasionally. Continuing to use Prism Percent after updates are posted means you accept the revised policy.
