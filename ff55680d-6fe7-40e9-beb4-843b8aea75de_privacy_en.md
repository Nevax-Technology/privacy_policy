## 2. How the App Handles Your Data

**Money Mind is a local-first application.** The data you enter — such as savings goals, deposits, and profile details — is stored **only on your device**. We do not transmit, upload, or sync your personal savings data to any external server or cloud service.

Your data stays on your device and remains under your full control.

### 2.1 Data You Enter

When you use the App, you may choose to enter the following information, which is saved locally on your device:

- **Profile Information:** Your display name and profile image.
- **Savings Goals:** Goal titles, target amounts, target dates, priority levels, categories, and personal notes.
- **Deposits:** Deposit amounts, optional notes, and optional evidence images you attach to deposits.
- **Achievements:** Badge progress and unlock history, generated based on your saving activity.
- **Feedback:** Ratings (overall, usability, design, and features) and optional comments you submit through the in-app feedback feature.
- **Preferences:** Language, appearance theme, notification settings, and reminder time preferences.

All of this data is stored locally on your device. It is not sent to us or to any third party.

### 2.2 Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following on your device's local storage:

- **Savings goals, deposit records, and achievement badge progress** — stored in a local SQLite database on your device.
- **Profile information and app settings** — stored in your device's local preferences storage.
- **Evidence images attached to deposits** — stored as files in the App's private documents directory.
- **Profile avatar image** — stored as a file in the App's private documents directory.

Because this data resides only on your device:

- **Uninstalling the App or clearing app data will permanently delete all of your information.**
- We cannot access, recover, or restore your data if your device is lost, reset, or damaged.
- You are encouraged to use device-level backup solutions if you wish to protect your data.

### 2.3 Third-Party Services

The App integrates third-party services that operate independently and may collect limited technical data from your device:

- **Advertising (Google AdMob):** We display advertisements through Google AdMob in three placements:
  - **App-Open Ads:** Shown when you launch or return to the App (with at least 15 minutes between showings).
  - **Interstitial Ads:** Shown after certain actions, such as creating a goal or adding a deposit.
  - **About-Page Banner:** A banner ad displayed on the About screen.

  Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. Ads are only loaded and displayed when you have granted consent (or are in a region where consent is not required). Ads are also suppressed when your device is offline. You can manage your preferences at any time through the App's **Legal & Privacy** settings.

- **Analytics (Firebase Analytics):** We use Firebase Analytics to understand general App usage patterns, including crash reports and performance data. This helps us improve the App's stability and reliability. Analytics events only contain anonymised metadata (such as event type, priority level, or badge name) and **never** include your goal titles, savings amounts, deposit values, profile information, or any other personal content. Analytics data collection is only active when you have granted consent (or are in a region where consent is not required), and is suppressed when your device is offline.

These third-party services are governed by their own privacy policies. They do **not** have access to your savings goals, deposits, profile, evidence images, feedback content, or any other personal content you store within the App.

## 3. How Your Data Is Used

### 3.1 Data Stored Locally

The data you enter is used exclusively to provide the App's features directly on your device:

- Displaying your goals, progress, and dashboard statistics.
- Tracking deposits and calculating savings milestones and progress pace.
- Detecting behind-schedule goals and triggering local reminders.
- Awarding achievement badges based on your saving activity.
- Sending local reminders and notifications based on your preferences:
  - **Daily Reminder:** A summary of your active savings goals.
  - **Due-Soon Alert:** When a goal's target date is approaching (within 3 days by default).
  - **Behind-Schedule Alert:** When your deposit progress falls behind the expected pace.
- Showing your profile name and avatar within the App.

This data never leaves your device.

### 3.2 Third-Party Service Data

Limited technical data collected by third-party services is used for:

- Serving and displaying advertisements within the App.
- Analysing App performance, stability, and usage trends in aggregate.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** share, sell, rent, or trade your personal savings data — goals, deposits, profile, images, or feedback — with any third party. This data remains on your device only.

The following limited data sharing may occur through third-party services:

- **Advertising Partners (Google AdMob):** Our advertising provider may receive non-personal, device-level data (such as device type and advertising identifier) to serve ads. This does not include any of your savings or profile data.
- **Analytics Providers (Firebase Analytics):** Anonymised, aggregate usage data may be shared with analytics services to help us understand general App performance. Analytics events only contain metadata (such as event type and priority level) — never personal information.
- **Legal Requirements:** In the unlikely event we are required by law, regulation, or legal process to disclose information, we would comply to the extent required.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Control Over Your Data

Because your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times:

- **View:** You can see all your data directly within the App.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or other regions with applicable data protection laws (including regulated US states such as California):

- You can **withdraw or change your consent** at any time through the **Legal & Privacy** section in the App's settings.
- You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data for advertising purposes.
- Your consent choices are remembered across sessions.

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

- Know what personal information is collected by third-party services operating within the App.
- Request deletion of your personal information.
- Opt out of the sale of personal information. **We do not sell your personal information.**
- Equal service and price when exercising your privacy rights.
