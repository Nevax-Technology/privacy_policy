# Privacy Policy for Subtitle Caption Studio

### Effective Date: December 9, 2025

### Last Modified: 2026-03-07

**1. Introduction**

Welcome to Subtitle Caption Studio, developed by NEXRAVEN INNOVATION. Subtitle Caption Studio is an AI-powered video and audio subtitle generation app for users aged 18 and above. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our app.

**2. Information We Collect**

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any credentials.

_2.1 Anonymous Authentication_

When you launch the app, we automatically sign you in anonymously using a Google-provided authentication service. This creates a random, anonymous user ID (UID) that is not linked to your real-world identity. No account creation, email address, or password is required. This UID is used solely to authorise your requests to our backend services and is never shared with third parties for identification purposes.

_2.2 Media Files_

To generate subtitles, the video or audio file you select is securely transmitted to our cloud backend for processing. We accept files up to 1 GB and 15 minutes in length. The file is processed temporarily and deleted from our servers immediately after processing is complete. We do not retain, analyse, or share your media files beyond what is strictly necessary to deliver the subtitle generation result.

_2.3 Feedback (Voluntary)_

If you choose to submit feedback through the About tab, your star rating and optional comment are stored in our cloud database, hosted on a Google-provided service. Submitted feedback is associated with your anonymous UID only. No personally identifiable information is stored alongside your feedback.

_2.4 Usage Data and Crash Reports_

The app may send limited crash and diagnostic data to our platform to help us identify and fix technical issues. This data does not include your media content or any personal information.

_2.5 Advertising Identifiers_

The app displays advertisements served by **Google AdMob**, including app-open ads, interstitial ads, rewarded ads, and banner ads. AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising identifier (e.g., Android Advertising ID) and other non-personal usage data to deliver relevant ads in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). You can reset or opt out of personalised ads at any time through your device settings.

**3. How We Use Your Information**

| Data                    | Purpose                                                    |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Anonymous UID           | Authenticate backend requests to our Google-hosted backend |
| Media files (temporary) | Generate subtitles via cloud AI speech-to-text             |
| Feedback (voluntary)    | Improve the app and understand user experience             |
| Crash/diagnostic data   | Identify and resolve technical issues                      |
| Advertising identifiers | Serve ads via Google AdMob                                 |

**4. Server-Side Media Handling**

Your media file is transmitted over an encrypted connection (HTTPS) to our backend, hosted on Google infrastructure. The file is held in temporary storage only for the duration of the subtitle generation process. Once generation is complete or if the process fails, the file is automatically purged from our servers. We do not use your media content for any purpose other than generating the subtitles you requested.

**5. Google Services**

Subtitle Caption Studio uses several Google-provided cloud services for authentication, backend processing, app security, and data storage. These services are operated by Google and subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

**6. Data Security**

We protect your data using the following measures:

- All data in transit between the app and our backend is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
- Our Google-provided backend infrastructure includes app integrity verification to ensure that only our genuine app can interact with our services.
- Short-lived authentication tokens are used to secure every backend request and are automatically refreshed when they expire.
- Temporary media files are removed from our servers as soon as processing is complete.
- We do not store any sensitive personal data.

**7. Data Sharing**

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Google:** as described in sections 5 and 2.5 above.
- **Legal requirements:** if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process.

**8. Export and Third-Party Sharing**

When you choose to export a subtitle file, the app may invoke third-party apps on your device (e.g., your email client or WhatsApp) to deliver the file. We have no control over how these third-party apps handle data once the file is shared. Please review the privacy policies of any third-party services you use to share your subtitle files.

**9. User Responsibilities**

Users are responsible for ensuring that they have the necessary rights to process any media file they import into the app, and for using the app in compliance with applicable local laws and regulations. Subtitle Caption Studio is intended for users aged 18 and above.

**10. Subscription Management**

The app offers a free tier and a premium tier. Premium subscriptions are managed through the **Google Play Store**. You can update, change, or cancel your subscription at any time through your Google Play account settings. All refund requests are subject to the Google Play Store refund policy. Upon cancellation, you will continue to have access to premium features until the end of the current billing period. Subscriptions auto-renew at the end of each billing period unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date. We reserve the right to modify subscription terms at any time; changes will be communicated through the app.

**11. Rewarded Advertisements**

Free-tier users may watch a rewarded video advertisement to earn additional subtitle generation uses. Watching a rewarded ad is entirely voluntary. Ad delivery and related data collection are handled by Google as described in section 2.5. A daily cap on bonus uses may apply and is configurable by NEXRAVEN INNOVATION.

**12. Children's Privacy**

Subtitle Caption Studio is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it promptly.

**13. Changes to This Privacy Policy**

NEXRAVEN INNOVATION reserves the right to update or modify this privacy policy at any time. When we make material changes, the "Last Modified" date at the top of this policy will be updated. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

**14. Contact Us**

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at [contact@nexraven.net](mailto:contact@nexraven.net).

By using Subtitle Caption Studio, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.
