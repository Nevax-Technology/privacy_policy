**Effective date:** 2026-03-18  
**App:** Universe Translator  
**Operator:** NEXRAVEN INNOVATION

This document explains how we handle your information. We have written it in clear, simple language so it is easy to understand.

By downloading, installing, or using Universe Translator, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

---

## 1. Privacy overview

We respect your privacy and aim to keep data handling straightforward.

Universe Translator does **not** require you to create an account to use the app.

Some information stays only on your device, such as saved items and settings. Some information is sent to Google services, backend services, advertising partners, analytics providers, or reliability providers when needed to run app features or when you give consent.

---

## 2. Information we may collect

### A. Information you provide through the app

Depending on how you use Universe Translator, we may receive:

- text you enter for translation
- speech content captured when you use voice input
- images or text extracted from images when you use camera or gallery translation
- feedback, ratings, and optional comments you choose to send
- messages you send when you contact support

### B. Information collected automatically

We may receive limited information such as:

- app version and basic device information
- language selections and feature usage events
- error, crash, and diagnostic information
- advertising-related signals and identifiers, where allowed
- network status or app interaction information needed to operate features

### C. Information stored on your device

The app may store information locally on your device, including:

- bookmarks
- translation history
- phrasebook-related saved state or unlock state
- app settings and preferences
- privacy consent choices
- certain app data needed to support offline or faster use

---

## 3. How we use information

We use information to:

- provide translation, speech, and image-based features
- save your preferences and app choices
- show your bookmarks, history, and other saved content
- improve app quality, reliability, and performance
- measure general app usage when you allow analytics
- show advertising on supported online surfaces while still forwarding your privacy choices to provider consent settings
- respond to support requests and review feedback you send us
- protect the app, users, and related services from misuse or abuse

---

## 4. When information is shared

We may share information only when needed for the app to work, when you ask us to, or when the law requires it.

Examples include:

- **Google services or backend services** to process translations, speech-related features, image text handling, app security checks, app reviews, and other app functions
- **Advertising providers** to deliver ads and related measurement, with your saved privacy choices forwarded to their consent/settings layer
- **Analytics or reliability providers** to understand app performance, fix problems, and improve stability
- **Support or feedback systems** when you choose to send a rating, comment, or support message
- **Legal or safety reasons** if disclosure is required to comply with law, enforce rights, or protect users and the public

Third-party providers may process information under their own privacy terms. For example, some features rely on Google services, and Google may handle related information according to its own policies.

When this happens, some information may be processed or managed in the **United States (US region)** by Google services or other trusted service providers that help operate the app.

## 5. Your privacy choices

You control important privacy options inside the app.

Depending on your choices, you can allow or reject categories such as:

- app usage analytics
- ad system data
- sharing data for advertising
- tailored advertising

You can also:

- stop using optional features that need permissions
- change device permissions in Android settings
- clear saved app data from your device
- uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data from your device
- choose not to send feedback or support messages

Please note: if you turn off some permissions or data uses, some features may be limited or unavailable.

## 6. Data retention

We keep different types of information for different lengths of time.

- **Local app data** such as bookmarks, history, settings, and consent choices generally stays on your device until you remove it, clear the app's data, or uninstall the app.
- **Feedback, diagnostics, analytics, ad-related data, and service logs** may be kept for as long as reasonably needed to operate the service, improve the app, meet legal obligations, prevent abuse, and resolve disputes.
