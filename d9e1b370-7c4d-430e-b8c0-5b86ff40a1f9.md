# Privacy Policy — Aura PDF

**Last Updated:** February 27, 2026
**Developer:** NEXRAVEN INNOVATION
**Contact:** contact@nexraven.net

---

## 1. Introduction

Aura PDF ("the App") is a local document utility that converts, merges, and processes PDF and image files entirely on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the controls available to you.

By using Aura PDF, you agree to the practices described below. You can review and update your consent preferences at any time from **Settings → Data & Privacy → Privacy Consent**.

---

## 2. Our Core Privacy Principle

> **All document processing happens on your device. Your files are never uploaded to any server.**

Whether you are online or offline, the conversion, merging, and pagination engines operate 100% locally. We have no access to your documents, their contents, or their filenames.

---

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ File names, file contents, or document data of any kind
- ❌ Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Photos, images, or PDFs processed within the App

### 3.2 Information We May Collect (Subject to Your Consent)

#### a) Anonymous Usage Analytics

If you grant **Analytics Storage** consent, we collect anonymised, non-personal app-usage events, such as:

- Types of operations performed (e.g. conversions, merges)
- Feature adoption signals (e.g. pagination toggle usage)
- General network context on launch (online vs. offline)

These events contain **no filenames, no file contents, and no personally identifiable information (PII)**.

#### b) Crash & Error Reports

We collect anonymised crash and error reports to help us diagnose and fix stability issues. Reports may include:

- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system version
- App version and build number
- Code-level error context (class/method names and non-PII counters)

Crash reports **never** include filenames, document contents, or any user-identifiable information.

#### c) Anonymous Device Identifier

We assign each installation a randomly generated, opaque anonymous identifier. This identifier:

- Contains no PII — it is a random string with no connection to your identity
- Is used solely to correlate voluntary feedback submissions from the same device
- Is stored locally on your device
- Is never linked to your name, email, or any other personal information

#### d) User Feedback

If you voluntarily submit feedback via **Settings → About → Rate Us**, the following data is recorded:

- Timestamp (UTC)
- App version string
- Star ratings (Overall, Ease of Use, Feature Satisfaction, Performance) — each a number from 1–5
- Optional free-text comment (maximum 300 characters)
- Your anonymous device identifier (omitted if unavailable)

Feedback is **entirely voluntary**. No feedback is collected silently or without explicit user action.

## 4. Advertising

Aura PDF displays advertisements when the device is online.

- **Banner ads** are displayed at the bottom of the main screen when online.
- **Interstitial ads** are shown after a successful file export/save action.
- **When you are offline, all ads are completely suppressed** — no ad network calls are made.

Ad display is subject to your **Ad Storage**, **Ad User Data**, and **Ad Personalisation** consent flags. You can opt out of personalised advertising by disabling these flags in **Settings → Data & Privacy → Privacy Consent**.

Google's privacy policy governs any data they collect: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

---

## 5. Your Privacy Consent Controls

On first launch, a **Privacy Consent screen** is presented before any data is collected or any ad is served. You must explicitly confirm your preferences before proceeding.

You can review and update these preferences at any time from **Settings → Data & Privacy → Privacy Consent**.

| Consent Flag           | Default | What It Controls                                       |
| ---------------------- | ------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Analytics Storage**  | ✅ On   | Whether usage analytics may be stored and transmitted  |
| **Ad Storage**         | ✅ On   | Whether ad-related storage is permitted                |
| **Ad User Data**       | ✅ On   | Whether user data may be used for ad targeting         |
| **Ad Personalisation** | ✅ On   | Whether personalised (targeted) advertising is enabled |

All four flags default to **opted-in**. Changing any flag takes effect immediately — no restart required.

---

## 6. Offline Mode & Data Suppression

Aura PDF monitors your network state continuously. When your device goes offline:

- 🚫 All analytics events are **silently dropped** — nothing is queued or deferred for later transmission.
- 🚫 All advertising calls are **suppressed**.
- ✅ The core document engine continues to operate normally.
- ✅ A visible **"✨ Offline Mode: Ad-Free"** indicator is displayed on the dashboard.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Aura PDF is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has submitted data through the App, please contact us at **contact@nexraven.net** and we will take steps to delete it promptly.

## 10. Data Security

- All document processing is performed **locally on your device** — your files are never transmitted over a network.
- On-device preferences are protected by the operating system's application sandbox.
- Analytics and crash data are transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.
- We do not operate any servers that store raw user files.
