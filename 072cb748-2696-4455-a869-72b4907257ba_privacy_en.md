# Privacy Policy for Task Pulse

**Effective Date:** April 21, 2026

## 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains, in general terms, how Task Pulse handles information when you use the app.

We aim to keep Task Pulse simple, respectful of user choice, and focused on helping you manage reminders and tasks.

## 2. Information We Handle

Task Pulse may handle information you choose to add or manage in the app, such as task details, reminder schedules, preferences, backup files, support requests, and feedback you choose to send.

We may also receive limited information related to app use, service availability, store features, advertising, reviews, updates, or general app improvement when those parts of the app are used.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information only for reasonable app-related purposes, such as:

- providing reminder and task features
- saving your preferences and choices
- supporting backup and restore actions you request
- improving reliability, quality, and user experience
- responding to support questions or feedback
- supporting store-related, advertising, review, or update features when available

## 4. Google and Other Service Providers

Task Pulse may use Google as a service provider for services connected to app distribution, store features, updates, reviews, advertising, and related app operations.

When you use those features, Google may process information under its own terms, privacy practices, and policies. We may also work with other service providers when needed to support the app experience, but only in connection with reasonable app-related purposes.

## 5. Your Choices and Control

You can choose what information you enter into Task Pulse. You can also manage many app-related permissions and settings through your device and through the app experience where available.

If you choose not to grant certain permissions or not to use certain connected services, some features may be limited or may not work as expected.

You are responsible for reviewing and managing any information you export, back up, restore, or share using your device or third-party services.

## 6. Data Retention and Security

We aim to handle information only for as long as it is reasonably needed for app-related purposes, legal obligations, support needs, or service operations.

We take reasonable steps to support a safe and reliable experience, but no app, device, or online service can guarantee absolute security. You are also responsible for keeping your device, accounts, and exported files secure.

## 7. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the updated version becomes effective on the stated effective date.

Your continued use of Task Pulse after changes take effect means you acknowledge the updated Privacy Policy.
