## 1. Introduction

Resize Ease is a free image resizing, cropping, and conversion utility for Android. We are
committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it
is used, and what choices you have.

**Core principle:** All image processing runs entirely on your device. Your images are **never
uploaded to any server, never stored in the cloud, and never shared with any third party.** No
account or registration is required to use this app.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Resize Ease app published on the Google Play Store
by NEXRAVEN INNOVATION.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do **not** collect or transmit:

- Your photos, images, or any image content
- Image filenames or file paths
- Any personally identifiable information such as name, email, or phone number
- Location data
- Contacts or address book data
- Microphone or camera data
- Browsing history or web activity

### 2.2 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored **only on your device** and never leaves your device:

| Data                        | Purpose                                                        |
| --------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Privacy consent preferences | Records which data collection categories you have consented to |
| Language preference         | Your selected app display language                             |
| App usage preferences       | Settings and preferences you configure within the app          |

### 2.3 Device Permissions

The app requests only the following device permissions:

| Permission      | Purpose                                                                          |
| --------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Storage (read)  | Selecting source images via the system file picker (older Android versions only) |
| Storage (write) | Saving output files to your chosen folder (older Android versions only)          |
| Internet access | Loading ads and sending analytics / crash reports (only when consent is granted) |

On **Android 10 and above**, saving files uses the standard Android folder-picker — no broad
storage permission is needed or requested on these versions.

**The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other
sensitive device resource.**

### 2.4 Analytics Data (Optional — Consent Required)

If you grant consent for **App Usage Analytics**, we collect anonymous, aggregated app usage
events via **Google Firebase Analytics**. No personal data, image content, filenames, or device
identifiers are included in any analytics event.

Analytics collection is **disabled by default** and only activated after you explicitly grant
consent. You may withdraw consent at any time via **Settings → Privacy** within the app.

### 2.5 Crash Reporting

The app uses **Google Firebase Crashlytics** to receive crash reports and error logs to help us
identify and fix bugs. Reports contain basic technical information such as device model, OS
version, app version, and error details. No personal information, image content, or filenames are
included.

### 2.6 Advertising Data (Optional — Consent Required)

The app displays ads via **Google AdMob**. When you interact with ads, Google AdMob may collect
advertising identifiers and general device information to serve and measure ads. The extent of
this collection depends on the consent choices you make in **Settings → Privacy**.

All ad-related consent flags default to **off (denied)**. You may change them at any time via
**Settings → Privacy**.

### 2.7 Feedback Submission (Optional — Requires Network)

The app includes an optional **Feedback** feature accessible from **About → Feedback**. If you
choose to submit feedback, your star rating, an optional comment, and the submission time are
sent to and stored on **Google Cloud**.

- Feedback submission is **entirely voluntary**.
- No name, email address, account, or device identifier is automatically attached to your
  feedback. However, if you include personal information in your comment, it will be stored
  as written.
- Feedback data is used solely to improve the app and is not shared beyond Google's cloud
  infrastructure.
- **To request deletion of feedback you have submitted**, contact us at **contact@nexraven.net**
  with the approximate submission date, and we will delete it within 30 days.

## 6. User Data Deletion

### 6.1 Local Data (On Your Device)

**To delete all local app data stored on your device:**

1. Go to your device's **Settings → Apps → Resize Ease**.
2. Tap **Clear Data** — removes all saved preferences.
3. Tap **Clear Cache** — removes any temporary processed image files.

Alternatively, **uninstalling the app** removes all local data automatically.

### 6.2 Feedback Submissions

To request deletion of feedback you have submitted, email us at **contact@nexraven.net** with the
approximate submission date. We will delete it within **30 days** of a verified request.

### 6.3 Google-Held Data (Analytics, Crash Reports, Ads)

Analytics events and crash reports sent to Google are anonymised and not linked to your identity.
To manage or delete data held by Google's services, visit:
[myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy](https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy)

## 7. Security & Data Transmission

- **Images:** All image processing runs entirely on your device. Images **never transit over any
  network connection.**
- **Local storage:** Locally stored data is protected by the device's application sandbox and is
  not accessible to other apps.
- **All outbound data:** Any data transmitted to Google's servers is sent over **encrypted (HTTPS)
  connections**.
- We do not operate any proprietary backend server. The only server-side data we store is
  voluntary feedback submissions via Google's cloud service.
